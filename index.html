The Cost of Hacking

The London Hackspace is funded by your membership subscriptions & donations. We plan to build up a cash reserve of three months running cost before we can look at moving to a larger space. At the moment we can just about cover the rent but don't have enough money for larger expenses (repairs, more frequent tool replacements, better electronics test equipment, new carpet, ...) Please consider becoming a member or increasing your monthly subscription payment.

How You Can Help

  • Become a member today! You'll get 24/7 access, a storage box for your projects, and warm fuzzy feelings for supporting us. The Hackspace requires our members to contribute an average subscription of £20/month to thrive, but how much you pay is up to you. The absolute minimum is £5/month.
  • If you're already a member and can afford it, increase your monthly subscription payment by a few pounds.
  • Volunteer to run a workshop on your area of expertise, or help us keep our infrastructure in shape.
  • Let us know if there's something you'd like added to or changed in the Hackspace that would convince you to pay more each month.
